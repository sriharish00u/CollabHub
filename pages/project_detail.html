About Project

Our team is developing a state-of-the-art neural network specifically designed for early-stage lung cancer screening. By leveraging large datasets of CT scans and clinical history, we aim to provide a high-precision diagnostic tool for radiologists. We are currently in the optimization phase, focusing on reducing false positives while maintaining sensitivity.
